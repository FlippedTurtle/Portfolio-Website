About

Hey there!

Welcome to my blog, I’m working in the tech area since the beginning of 2008. I started my career in Second Life, I was building 3D environments for companies. In 2011 I switched to the web as a developer.

Then I spent 1.5 years in Silicon Valley where I really discovered the startup ecosystem and all the possibilities. I’m now an entrepreneur making a living from my online products.

This blog is a gathering of all the news, thoughts, opinions I shared during this decade as a tech worker (in french for the older post, in English since 2018)

My current projects are:

  • LeadLeadBangBang: A productivized agency. We send emails so our clients get meetings
  • RemoteFR: A job board dedicated to full remote jobs for French devs
  • FocusPlus: A Pomodoro Timer that keeps your flow alive
  • SaaS boilerplate: Un annuaire des meilleurs boilerplates pour monter son SaaS
  • RoxNation: Un annuaire des gyms français proposant de l’hyrox
